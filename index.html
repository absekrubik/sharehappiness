Who We Are

About ShareHappiness

Building a brighter future through acts of kindness and community support

Our Mission

To spread happiness and improve lives through charitable giving, volunteer efforts, and community engagement across Australia.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to basic needs, education, and the opportunity to live with dignity and joy.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, inclusivity, and transparency guide everything we do, ensuring trust and impact in our communities.

Our Story

ShareHappiness was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate Nepalese living in Australia who believed that small acts of kindness could create ripples of positive change. What started as a local food drive in Sydney has grown into a nationwide movement touching thousands of lives.

Today, we work with communities across Australia, partnering with local organizations, volunteers, and donors to address critical needs in food security, education, mental wellness, and community development.

Every day, our dedicated team and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that happiness isn't just a feeling, but a tangible reality for those who need it most.

What We Do

Our Programs

Making a difference through focused initiatives and community-driven projects

Food Security Drives

Providing nutritious meals and food packages to families in need across metropolitan and regional Australia.

  • Weekly food distributions
  • Emergency meal support
  • Community kitchens

Education Support

Empowering students through scholarships, tutoring programs, and educational resources for underprivileged youth.

  • Scholarship programs
  • Free tutoring services
  • School supply donations

Mental Wellness Campaigns

Supporting mental health through awareness programs, counseling services, and community support groups.

  • Free counseling sessions
  • Wellness workshops
  • Support group networks

Community Development

Building stronger communities through skills training, employment support, and neighborhood improvement projects.

  • Skills workshops
  • Job placement assistance
  • Community events
